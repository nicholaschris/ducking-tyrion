Abacus Finch

The teaching community

Welcome to Abacus Finch. Abacus Finch aims to bring teachers in South Africa closer together through providing an online community platform. The platform will focus on providing news and opinions on education technology. Additionally, online professional development courses for educators will be available. The online courses will promote the use of technology in the classroom and eventually educators will feel comfertable enough with ed-tech solutions to make use of them in the classroom.
